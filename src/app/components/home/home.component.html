Rules Rule 1 : Job Selection

When choosing a job, please do not choose a job that is not your area of expertise or a job outside of your field of work. If you see a worder that you do not have expertise or outside of your field of work in the "Work Orders" table, click on "Profile" from the menu on the left and update your job type and work area selections.

 Rule 2 : Be Gentle

Be polite to the customer while doing the job. Worderize is very strict in this regard and takes customer feedback into consideration.

 Rule 3 : Drive Safe

Use the vehicle carefully while going to worder address. Worderize does not want any worker to be harmed.

 Rule 4 : Customer Privacy

After finishing the WORDER, do not contact the customer who gave the work order or do not save the customer’s information.

 Rule 5 : Let us know

If you selected a worder that is somewhere you cannot go or have a job type that you cannot do, access our system as soon as possible. Do not continue by getting a new job by clicking the “Done” button in the "Your Current Job" section.
