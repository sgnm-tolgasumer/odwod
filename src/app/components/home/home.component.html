Rules Rule 1 : Job Creation

While creating a WORDER, please do not give WORDER which is impossible to done. Please select appropriate job field according to your WORDER, otherwise you cannot find any worker to get your job done.

 Rule 2 : Be Gentle

Be polite to the workers while they are doing their job. Worderize is very strict in this regard and takes user feedback into consideration.

 Rule 3 : Do Not Mix The Work Orders

When any worker come to do WORDER you give, please do not ask for any other job because worker's schedule may not available for additional jobs so create another WORDER instead.
